Data Deletion Request

Last updated: April 25, 2026

You have the right to request deletion of your Proxie account and all associated personal data at any time. We offer two ways to do this.

Option 1 — Delete from the App

The fastest way. No email needed.

1
Open Proxie and sign in Make sure you're signed into the account you want to delete.
2
Go to your Profile tab Tap the person icon in the bottom navigation bar.
3
Open Settings Tap the gear icon in the top right of your profile.
4
Tap "Delete Account" Scroll to the bottom of Settings. Confirm twice when prompted. Your account and all data will be permanently deleted.

Option 2 — Email Request

If you can't access the app or logged in via Facebook/Google and want us to handle deletion manually:

Send an email to:

housexdevore@gmail.com

Subject line: Account Deletion Request

Include the email address or phone number associated with your Proxie account. We'll confirm deletion within 5 business days.

What Gets Deleted

  • Your profile (name, bio, photo, rating)
  • All listings you posted
  • Your saved items and collections
  • Your account credentials

Messages may be retained for up to 90 days after deletion for safety and dispute resolution purposes, then permanently removed.

Deletions are processed within 30 days of request. You will receive a confirmation email once complete.
